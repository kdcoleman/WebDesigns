ABOUT ME

WHY I MADE THIS BLOG?

I've created this site as a space where I can talk about the things that interest me and share them with others. I've spent the last year trying to figure out a way to combine my passion for dance and my love of fashion and beauty products. Well here it is! I've created this blog to mainly serve as a self-motivator, but all the beautiful people out there who are willing to listen to what I have to say are welcome. It was time for me to stop waiting around and take action, so this is me breaking out of my comfort zone and diving in with no inhibitions. I'm excited for the journey ahead, and I hope I'll inspire you to embark on a journey of your own.
