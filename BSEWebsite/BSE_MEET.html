Bridgette Still
Certified Wedding Planner

Hi I'm Bridgette, Lead Planner and Designer... the "B" behind B. Still Events. We are a full service wedding and event planning company based in North Carolina, but love to travel wherever love takes us.

As your planner, I'm here to share my knowledge and experience while working side by side with you on every detail leading up to your special day. I love planning and designing unique and memorable events that reflect your personality and individual style. At BSE, we are committed to ensuring your event is executed flawlessly and your guests leave with an unforgettable experience.

Planning an event should be exciting and stress free. Let us take care of the details so you can relax and enjoy every moment of the experience. By the end of your event, we'll feel like family!


Kayla | Event Assistant

Meet Event Assistant and the love of my life, my daughter, Kayla. If you ask her, she will probably tell you about all of the elaborate birthday parties I planned for her as a child. As a teenager, she began to design and plan her own events. Her keen attention to detail, eye for style and creativity are valuable to our team. Kayla's infectious smile and sweet spirit make each client feel calm yet excited about their special day.

Kayla is currently a Ph.D. student at North Carolina State University majoring in Applied Mathematics. Yes, I'm a proud mom! When she's not assisting with weddings or studying, you can find her in the dance studio taking class, teaching choreography, or performing.
